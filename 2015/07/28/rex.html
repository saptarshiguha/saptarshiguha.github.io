Rex

Jul 28, 2015
Enough said. I have phantom Rex syndrome. When I drive I look back to see a missing Rex. When I wake up, I look down to see a missing Rex. When I walk around the home, I hear a missing panting Rex.
We sometimes look after Rex when his owner is not in town. He went back to his owner yesterday ...


Rex stayed us for two weeks. When I see his face, I'm filled with feelings of love. But I also remember the amount of work required, like taking him to the park, feeding him, getting him bathed and keeping a note on dog sitters. It's worth it. Pictures with XT1 and the 35mm/1.4 lens (edited in Lightroom) and some are with Tmax 400 while I strolled around San Francisco dog nearby.
